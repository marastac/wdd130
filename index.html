Cajamarca

Cajamarca is a city in the mountainous region of the Andes in Peru. It is known for its rich history, beautiful landscapes, and natural hot springs. The city was an important center during the Inca period and is famous for the capture of the Inca emperor Atahualpa by the Spanish conquistadors.
